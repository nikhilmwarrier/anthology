<p>Designed for a fast, clean reading experience.</p>
<b>Features</b>
<ul>
  <li>OLED-friendly reader mode.</li>
  <li>Brightness can go below 0%.</li>
  <li>A minimal overlay gives you access to a clock and navigation.</li>
  <li>Includes convenient links to Project Gutenberg and Standard Ebooks.</li>
  <li>Free and open-source (AGPLv3).</li>
</ul>
<p>
  Note: Anthology is still in its early stages of development. Expect the usual
  number of bugs and glitches.
</p>
